About Us

TinDog is designed to cater to the needs of dog owners and their dogs. To solve the dilemma within the contemporary social scene, which was the lack of platforms, has always been its core concept. TinDog saw and captured this opportunity. Over 1 million users encounter each other, and 10 thousands matches are taking place daily on TinDog.

As an easy-to-navigate yet pressure-free platform, the app has forever changed how users with similar interests meet and socialize with one another. By giving the people within its community more control and space to choose whom they like, TinDog has won over hundreds of thousands of users' hearts. With 300 thousands registered users and more than 10 thousands daily active users, it is the hottest social app to get a hold of.
